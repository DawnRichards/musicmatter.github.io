About




Dawn Richards has been playing the violin since she was six and achieved grade 8 violin in 2015. She also has grade 8 singing and grade 5 theory. During her time at school Dawn was involved in many clubs, including two choirs, leading the string chamber group and school orchestra, training a younger string group, and other ad hoc groups. As a music prefect and scholar, she has also been involved in stage managing concerts and assisting the teachers generally within the department.

About Me

In the summer of 2014 Dawn completed two weeks of work experience at the artist management company, IMG Artists, where she was able to witness the organization that goes on behind the big classical music stars.
